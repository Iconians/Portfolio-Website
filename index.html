The Development Process

1st Step

The first step in the development process which is probably the hardest is to sit down and figure out what kind of website you want, if you want a landing page with a few pieces of content or a website that has multiple pages, then you need to decide how you want your site to look (the layout) making a sketch is helpful when trying to visualize, then when you sit down with me we can refine the sketch and if you need further help in finding the right layout you can visit different sites you like and look at how the content is layed out which can help you figure what layout you like then of course I as the technical partner can assist you in refining the sketch and bounce ideas around.

2nd Step

The second step in the process is scheduling a free initial meeting with the me (the technical partner) which will last 10 to 15 minutes, which we will discuss an overview of your project and decide if we will work well together, then we will set up a free 45 min to an hour meeting and dig into the project and figure out all the nuts and bolts and put it down on paper and signed, at this point the initial payment is due to start development process.

3rd Step

the third step is the actual development, this is where I look over the sketch and divide the sketch into sections and group content based on location and other commonalities, then think about what tools to use that make the most sense when the site is viewed on a phone or tablet since most responsive tools are built into the desktop version of the site then as the screen gets smaller like on a tablet or phone there will only be minimal adjustments needed, then I build the site out and will send a picture to the client for their feedback and then will implement their adjustments and once the client is satisfied I will upload the site to the clients server

4th Step

the fourth step is collect final payment if there is one and for the client to decide if they wish to keep me as their technical partner for future website updates
